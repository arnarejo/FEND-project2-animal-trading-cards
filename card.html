Tyrannosaurus is a genus of coelurosaurian theropod dinosaur. It is one of the most well-represented of the large theropods.

  • Scientific name: Tyrannosaurus
  • Speed: 19 km/h
  • Extinction status: Extinct
  • Earliest fossil record: Cretaceous

Tyrannosaurus rex is one of the most well-represented of the large theropods. Tyrannosaurus lived throughout what is now western North America, on what was then an island continent known as Laramidia. It was the last known member of the tyrannosaurids.
